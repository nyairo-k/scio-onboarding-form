Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the SCIO Expert Network. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you apply to join our network through our website form.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal and professional information from you during the application process:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your full name and email address.
  • Professional Information: Your LinkedIn profile URL and the contents of your CV/Resume, which may include your address, languages spoken, work experience, skills, and qualifications.
  • Network Information: How you heard about us and whether you have previously worked with SCIO (including project details if applicable).

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and evaluate your application for our expert network.
  • To contact you regarding your application and potential project opportunities.
  • To manage our expert database and for internal record-keeping.
  • To facilitate ongoing engagement, including periodic updates to your availability and CV.

4. AI-Powered Analysis

As part of our screening process, we utilize Artificial Intelligence (AI) to analyze your CV. This process helps us automatically extract key details such as your skills, experience, and qualifications to streamline our evaluation. We require your explicit consent for this analysis. The results of the AI analysis are always supplemented by a manual review by our team.

5. Data Storage and Security

All submitted information is stored securely in our Supabase database. Your CV file is stored in a secure Supabase Storage bucket. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information will only be used for the purposes of managing the SCIO Expert Network and for potential project placements, which may involve sharing your profile with trusted clients after obtaining your consent for a specific opportunity.

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so, please contact us at the email address provided below.
